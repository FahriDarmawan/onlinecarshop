Automabiles Of the year
Motor Trend

The Motor Trend Car of the Year awards are awarded to the best new or significantly refreshed car in a given model year. Each vehicle is evaluated against six key criteria: Design Advancement, Engineering Excellence, Efficiency, Safety, Value and Performance of Intended Function. Debuting in 1949, this award is one of the most prestigious honors in the auto industry. With a monthly circulation of over 1 million each month, Motor Trend is one of the top national automotive magazines.

Residual Value Awards, Residual Value Awards
ALG

ALG is an industry benchmark for residual values and depreciation data. ALG Residual Value Awards honor vehicles in over 20 segments that are predicted to retain the highest percentage of their original price after a three-year period. These awards are derived after careful study of segment competition, historical vehicle performance, and industry trends. Award segments include Mainstream Brand, Luxury Brand, Entry Compact car, Full-size Pickup, and Sporty Car, among many others.
